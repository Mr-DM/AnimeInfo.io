Jujutsu Kaisen

Synopsis: Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese anime series based on the manga of the same name by Gege Akutami. It follows the story of Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes involved in the world of curses and sorcery after ingesting a powerful cursed object.

Information: Jujutsu Kaisen has gained immense popularity since its serialization began in 2018. The anime adaptation premiered in 2020 and has received critical acclaim for its animation, storytelling, and character development.
