Solo Leveling

Synopsis: Solo Leveling is a South Korean web novel written by Chugong. It follows the story of Sung Jin-Woo, the weakest hunter, who gains the ability to level up in strength and skills, embarking on a journey to become the strongest hunter.

Information: Solo Leveling has gained immense popularity since its release, known for its engaging storyline and character development. The webtoon adaptation has also received critical acclaim for its art and storytelling.
