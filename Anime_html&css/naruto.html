Naruto

Synopsis: Naruto is a Japanese anime series based on the manga of the same name by Masashi Kishimoto. It follows the journey of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with a sealed demon fox spirit within him, as he seeks recognition and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.

Information: Naruto has been serialized since 1999 and has become one of the best-selling manga series of all time. The anime adaptation began airing in 2002 and has since released over 700 episodes.
