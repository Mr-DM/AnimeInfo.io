One Piece

Synopsis: One Piece is a Japanese anime series based on the manga of the same name by Eiichiro Oda. It follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew in their quest to find the legendary One Piece treasure and become the Pirate King.

Information: One Piece has been serialized since 1997 and has become one of the best-selling manga series of all time. The anime adaptation began airing in 1999 and has since released over 1000 episodes.
