Bleach

Synopsis: Bleach is a Japanese anime series based on the manga of the same name by Tite Kubo. It follows the story of Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager who gains the abilities of a Soul Reaper and is tasked with protecting the living world from evil spirits.

Information: Bleach has been serialized since 2001 and has become one of the most popular manga series. The anime adaptation began airing in 2004 and has since released over 360 episodes.
